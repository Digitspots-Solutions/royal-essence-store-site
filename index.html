About Us

Royal Essence Store is Ibadan's premier destination for fashion and creative design excellence. We specialize in curating stunning ready-to-wear collections and delivering bespoke creative design services that capture your individual style. Our passion is helping you express your royal essence through fashion that makes a statement.

Whether you're looking for the latest trends, traditional attire with a modern twist, or custom designs for special occasions, our dedicated team brings years of expertise in fashion styling and creative design. We understand the Nigerian fashion landscape and blend international trends with local flair to create pieces that resonate with your lifestyle.

Our Services

Ready-to-Wear Collections

Custom Design & Tailoring
